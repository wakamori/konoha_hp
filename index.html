Scripting languages, such as JavaScript and Ruby, are booming and widely accepted in industries. Their dynamic features are very nice and offer you flexibility when you start coding. But, as your scripts grow larger, you would suffer from the lack of static programming features.

Do you want to rewrite your scripts to C++ or Java? Or, are you still trying to maintain bloated scripts by hands? Neither is so attractive answer.

Konoha is newly designed to deliver both flexible scripting and static programing with one single language. Your scripts are programmed smoothly, and serve well with a long-term maintenance.
